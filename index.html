The Renewable Energy Solution

Turning Poultry Litter into Power

Poultry Power USA generates and supplies electricity using poultry litter, a renewable resource. Our power plants offer a solution to the challenges of poultry litter disposal by utilizing advanced gasification technology to create clean renewable energy.

To reduce our carbon footprint, we chose a 36 MW plant configuration, allowing us to keep the poultry litter circle of acquisition to the smallest possible area.

Our power plants benefit all segments of the poultry industry, from the integrators down to the individual growers.

For Integrators and Growers

We offer many benefits for affiliates

  • We provide service to clean-out and re-bed poultry houses.
  • We offer a bedding lease program to lower capital expenditures for bedding.
  • We have a dedicated plant to provide pine shavings for bedding materials.
  • The sanitization facility is located inside plant to ensure no cross contamination.
  • Litter removal is via state-of-the-art equipment so that litter never touches the ground.
  • Bedding material placed in houses will not touch exterior ground.
  • Provide more frequent clean out of grower houses.
  • 365 day per year availability for clean out scheduling.
  • Byproduct of the gasification process is a biochar product that will be beneficial to farmers.
  • Poultry Power USA will accept liability of litter.
